
> **3. cut** 

âœ” Best Use Case: 
 Simple field extraction based on delimiters.

**Example**                   
name="teja rajesh kiran ganesh"                                 
age="35 25 63 25"                                                                              
place="bhimavaram goa hyd vizag"


```
#!/bin/bash
name="teja rajesh kiran ganesh"
age="35 25 63 25"
place="bhimavaram goa hyd vizag"

echo "hyy this is $(echo $name | cut -d ' ' -f3) and I am from $(echo $place | cut -d ' ' -f3) and my age is $(echo $age | cut -d ' ' -f3)"

````
