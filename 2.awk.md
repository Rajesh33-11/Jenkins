
> **2. awk** 

âœ” Best Use Case: 
  When working with logs or structured text.

**Example**                   
name="teja rajesh kiran ganesh"                                 
age="35 25 63 25"                                                                              
place="bhimavaram goa hyd vizag"



```
#!/bin/bash
name="teja rajesh kiran ganesh"
age="35 25 63 25"
place="bhimavaram goa hyd vizag"

echo "hyy this is $(echo $name | awk '{print $2}') and I am from $(echo $place | awk '{print $2}') and my age is $(echo $age | awk '{print $2}')"


````
