
> **4. loops** 

âœ” Best Use Case: 
  Automatically printing many rows or processing many values.

**Example**                   
name="teja rajesh kiran ganesh"                                 
age="35 25 63 25"                                                                              
place="bhimavaram goa hyd vizag"



```
#!/bin/bash#!/bin/bash

name="teja rajesh kiran ganesh"
age="35 25 63 25"
place="bhimavaram goa hyd vizag"

# Convert to arrays
names=($name)
ages=($age)
places=($place)

# Loop through array indexes
for i in ${!names[@]}
do
    echo "hyy this is ${names[$i]} and I am from ${places[$i]} and my age is ${ages[$i]}"
done


````
